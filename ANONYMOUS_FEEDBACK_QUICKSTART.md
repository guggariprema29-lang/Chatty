## â“ FAQ

**Q: Will they know it's me?**
A: No! The sender is completely anonymous. They cannot see who sent it.

**Q: Can I send multiple feedbacks?**
A: Only 1 per person per 24 hours. This prevents spam and encourages quality.

**Q: What if I receive mean feedback?**
A: Delete it! Click the trash icon. Report serious abuse to admins.

**Q: When does the 24-hour limit reset?**
A: 24 hours from when you sent the last feedback (not calendar day).

**Q: Can I send to myself?**
A: No, the system prevents self-feedback.

**Q: Can I reply to anonymous feedback?**
A: Not yet, but you can send them feedback back (if you guess who it's from!)
